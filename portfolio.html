About Me

I am Malahima Amir, a passionate BSc Software Engineering student with a growing expertise in web development. My skills include HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which I use to create interactive, user-friendly websites. I am constantly learning new technologies and improving my design and development capabilities.

Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a solid foundation in software engineering principles and practices. I have worked on various projects that have helped me hone my skills in both front-end and back-end development. My experience includes building responsive web applications, implementing user interfaces, and optimizing web performance to enhance user experience.

I am particularly interested in exploring frameworks like React and Angular, which allow for dynamic, single-page applications. In addition to web development, I have a keen interest in user experience (UX) design and understand the importance of creating intuitive and accessible designs that cater to all users.

I am actively participating in coding bootcamps and hackathons to further enhance my problem-solving abilities and collaborate with other tech enthusiasts. I believe in continuous learning and regularly engage with online courses and tutorials to keep myself updated with the latest trends in technology.

This portfolio showcases my dedication to building responsive and innovative web applications, reflecting my enthusiasm for software engineering. I am excited to connect with like-minded individuals and explore opportunities in the tech industry where I can contribute my skills and learn from experienced professionals.
